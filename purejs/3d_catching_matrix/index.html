Experiment steps:
  1. We are generating a promise that is already fulfilled or rejected.
  2. (Optionally) we are inserting something in between: then, catch or finally.
  3. Finally, we are invoking then, catch or finally and marking V if callback was invoked.
Important things to note:
  • 'Catch' ignores fulfilled promise, 'then' ignores rejected promise
  • 'Then' does not change promise state
  • 'Catch' returns fulfilled promise
  • 'Finally' will be invoked always!
  • 'Finally' does not change promise state
